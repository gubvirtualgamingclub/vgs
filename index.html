OUR CORE VALUES

Our Mission

The mission of GUCC Virtual Gaming Society is to build a thriving, inclusive community where students passionate about gaming can connect, compete, collaborate, and develop both their technical and strategic skills.

We aim to promote esports culture within the university environment, encouraging teamwork, leadership, sportsmanship, and creativity. Through tournaments, workshops, training sessions, and community engagement, we seek to empower gamers and help them explore career pathways in esports, game development, streaming, and beyond.

Our Vision

Our vision is to establish GUCC Virtual Gaming Society as a leading esports and gaming organization in the country, recognized for excellence in competitive gaming, innovation, and community development.

We envision a future where gaming is celebrated as a legitimate and empowering form of education, entertainment, and career opportunity. We aspire to foster the next generation of professional gamers, content creators, developers, and esports leaders from Green University and beyond.

Our Story

The GUCC Virtual Gaming Society (VGS) is the pioneering official esports and gaming club at Green University of Bangladesh. We are a vibrant and inclusive community of students united by a shared passion for competitive gaming, technological innovation, and creative expression. Our society serves as the central hub for gamers of all skill levels to connect, compete, and collaborate.


We are more than just a club; we are a movement. We aim to elevate the perception of gaming, transforming it from a casual hobby into a platform for skill development, strategic thinking, and career exploration. By organizing professional-grade tournaments, workshops, and community events, we provide our members with the resources and opportunities they need to excel.
